How to get involved in ACM-W

You're welcome to join ACM-W at any time during the school year! All genders are welcome at our meetings, and you can come to as many or as few of our meetings as you'd like throughout the semester.


We hold a range of events, from student panels, to technical workshops, to featured speakers, to networking sessions, to socials. To be notified about our events, please join our mailing list here and feel free to drop in on any meeting you're interested in. For any questions, reach out to acmwosu@gmail.com.
